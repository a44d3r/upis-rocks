Rose

The eldest of the three princesses of House Rosario, Rose was a responsible and clever girl who worked hard for what she wanted. Her one goal was to one day become a lawyer in the hopes that she can help as many people caught in the civil war as possible.

She always said she had no time for petty relationships and romance. Her dream of becoming a lawyer came before all of those.

Unfortunately, her friend Aguilar, fell in love with her.

On the outside, they always seemed to be bickering. Most of it was just Aguilar's way of courting her, however.
