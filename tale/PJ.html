PJ

Ryuu Ga Waga Teki Wo Ku Go Fund Yourself.

One half of the Al-Wakahalaishmail twins, PJ always kept things to himself, often locking himself up in his room to listen to edgy music.

He rarely went outside the castle and didn't socialize much with others.

He was close to two people inside the hold, however: his brother Riley, and the Chief Royal Guard Felipe, who taught them how to fight with swords.

It was there that PJ felt like he belonged for the first time. Tragedy struck, however, as Felipe died defending the hold from bandits.

He let out his anger in the forest by whacking trees. There, he accidentally hit a girl, Riza, who was passing by. She was wounded.

He offered to mend her wound, and as he did, they got to know each other better. PJ finally found someone else to talk to.

Whenever PJ mourned the loss of Felipe, he went to Riza for comfort.

As time passed, PJ started to develop feelings for Riza. Unfortunately, his brother Riley also had feelings for her.
