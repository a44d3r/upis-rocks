Moira

It's a Rosario thing.

Sister to Rose, and twin to Maria, Moira is the only one who actually acted like a princess. Prim and proper, she didn't swear, and was always calm and reserved. She was a bit clumsy, being especially flustered whenever Aguilar is around.

After learning that Aguilar was in love with Rose and that Maria was in love with him, Moira decided that she had feelings for Aguilar and confessed.

Aguilar stopped talking to her and started to avoid her. After two months of that, she decided to send him a letter. What could it possibly contain? Who knows...
