Riley

Alam n'yo may lagnat ako ngayon eh.

One half of the twin princes of Wakahalaishmail Hold, Riley grew up with only his brother PJ for company. Although they had conflicting personalities, they still remain very close.

He and his brother met their best friend, Raiden, when he was just a Royal-Guardsman-in-training. They challenged him to a clash of swords when they saw him in the Wakahalaishmail Castle.

As Riley became older, he got more into politics. He kept arguing with the people around him.

This brought him huge trouble one town fair. He got into a huge argument with random folk and got his ass whooped.

He was left bloodied and alone until a girl, Riza, helped him. She took care of him, helping him back on his feet. They became friends soon after.

Riley was a weird dude. He always did strange things, such as running on all fours or going into sewers with cats. All of that, he did for one reason: to get Riza's attention.

Riley, at one point, wanted to ask Riza to accompany him to the Jarl's Ball. He was disappointed, however, when all Riza told him was, "I'm sorry. I appreciate the effort, but no."

They high-fived each other awkardly twice and went off in separate directions.

Riley got over it (or so he says) and is now doing the same old shenanigans for the same reason.
