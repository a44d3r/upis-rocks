Jose

Brad gusto mo ba masapak bahay mo?

Jose or the Pack Mule was a man surrounded by an air of mystery. Nobody knows where he came from. One moment, there is nothing, and the next, a large man has suddenly appeared, standing there. Just... staring out in one direction.

He was found by Riza in their outhouse, eating stools.

They have been friends since that meeting. Jose rarely talked, but it was no hindrance to their friendship. He and Riza didn't need to talk to communicate. They had a deeper sort of connection.

Jose usually followed Riza, protecting her and carrying her stuff.
