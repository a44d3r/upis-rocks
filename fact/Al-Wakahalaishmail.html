Al-Wakahalaishmail Faction

"Do or do not, there is no try.

The first rulers of the Wakahalaishmailian lands, hence the name. These once great nobles fell from grace a few weeks after the tyrannous Smiths came and divided the lands.

After the first war between the Wakahalaishmailians and the Smiths, the citizens of this faction were left scarred. Most of them sought redemption, but no one dared cross the Smiths again after the massive casualties suffered in the war.

The heads of the royal family, the King and Queen, have fled to the wilderness along with a few guards. Leaving their two sons, Prince PJ and Prince Riley in charge of a broken and morale-drained kingdom.
