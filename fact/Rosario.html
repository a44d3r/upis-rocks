Rosario Faction

Honour above all.

The capital city of House Rosario was the center of all trade between the Wakahlaishmailian factions. A thriving city that many travelers used as a pit stop whenever they went to other lands.

The Rosario faction had lively citizens who all happily served their kingdom and the royal family.

The royal family consisted of the King and his three daughters. The Queen died of an unknown illness while on a business trip to the lands of the Smiths, though there were rumors that circulated around the kingdom that she was assassinated.
