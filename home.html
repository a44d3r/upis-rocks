UPIS ROCKS

Strangers from another land have arrived on the shores of the the Wakahalaishmailian country, their intentions seemingly benevolent. Little did the inhabitants know, these outsiders had far more sinister goals.

Led by the Smiths, they sought to subjugate the natives and take the land for themselves.

Civil unrest stirred throughout Wakahalaishmail. The tyrannous outsiders have caused turmoil, with the native Rosarios, Joneses, and Al-Wakahalaishmails seeking to drive off the invaders.

With the Smiths having the largest army, they were able to take most of the land, while the natives lost nearly all of what used to be their kingdom.

How were the denizens of this land gripped by conflict affected by the new order brought about by these newcomers? Read on and find out...
